About us

Stellar Stories.

We are a team of six enthusiastic students from MAMS, pursuing our BCA degrees. Our team members are Jerin Jaison, Aleeja Joji, Anna Sona Saju, Dhyan V. Dileep, Adhnan P. Ummar, and Diya Deepu. United by our passion for technology, space, and innovation, we have come together to participate in this exciting challenge, combining our skills in research, programming, and creativity to explore the mysteries of the cosmos.

We chose to participate in the NASA Space Apps Challenge because it offers a unique opportunity to apply scientific knowledge to real-world problems while pushing the boundaries of innovation. Our team, Stellar Stories, created a project called Story Stellar, which focuses on the journey of stars—from their formation in nebulae to their ultimate transformation into white dwarfs, supernovae, or black holes, and the resulting stardust that connects all life. To present this project, we developed a website using Bootstrap, combining visual storytelling with interactive content to make the stellar journey engaging and accessible. By participating in this hackathon, we aim to bridge science, technology, and creativity, enhance our problem-solving skills, and contribute meaningfully to a global initiative that celebrates curiosity, exploration, and discovery

MAMS

Marian Academy of Management Studies

We are students of the BCA program at Marian Academy of Management Studies (MAMS) in Kothamangalam, a renowned institution recognized for its commitment to academic excellence and innovative learning. Affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University and approved by AICTE, MAMS has established itself as a centre of higher education with a focus on quality, industry-oriented programs. As aspiring technology and management professionals, we benefit from its vision of fostering creativity, critical thinking, and practical skills, preparing us to contribute effectively to the evolving world of information technology and digital innovation.
